Backgauge

Z axis backgauge speed diff percent

Range

1 to 100

Default

20

Units

%

Access

Administrator

Function

When moving Z1 and Z2 together, the speed difference in percentage with which one BG should move. If moving left (considering operator is standing in front of the machine), Z2 will move slower than Z1. If moving right, Z1 will move slower than Z2.

Z-axes limited velocity

Range

1 to 33

Default

33

Units

Access

Standard

Function

Z-axis limited velocity controls the maximum speed of the backgauge’s movement along the Z-axis to ensure safe and precise positioning

Description

When the manual Z-axis is mounted on the machine, the back gauge operates at a slower speed to ensure precision and prevent potential damage
